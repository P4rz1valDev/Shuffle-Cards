HOW TO PLAY

The game is split into 4 main phases:

1. Memorize pattern of the cards!

2. Your cards will be shuffled and the opponent cards will be fliped!

3. Move the cards in the Right order using the 1-4 keys (left to right)!

4. Hit Enter to check the order of the cards, if you got every card in the right spot, you win, but if you got more than 2 cards wrong, then        you loose 1 life out of 3 an you have to try again.
